About Our Education
Nirman IAS is a premier learning institute, established in 2007 with the aim to mentor IAS aspirants with the right approach. We work meticulously and consistently on student’s strengths, as well as weaknesses, helping them realize and improve their hidden potential. We create an environment of inquisitiveness, which naturally paves way for creative, rational and scientific thinking. Going by the current wave of UPSC, merely mugging up of facts or concepts can never translate in to success. It mandates thorough assessment of an individual’s well-rounded personality and balanced bent of mind. Here lies our USP, we focus on ameliorating the analytical skills of an individual and develop their personality in a holistic way. Our programs are designed in such a way that diverse educational and social background of students works as an asset for every one, resulting in a peer learning environment, working at a subtle level. Highly experienced faculty and an enviable track record makes us first choice for any IAS aspirant.
Director's Message
An educational institute is not just about bricks, mortar, and concrete, but about building character, enriching minds, and enriching experiences that last a lifetime. Our Institute is a place that helps shape the future of its students. From the modern education system, styles may have changed, new technologies may have emerged, but a values-based education system remains the same. Over the years, we have continued to impart holistic education to our children, helping to nurture them mentally, emotionally, physically and spiritually. Through knowledge and skill every individual can achieve greatness. The common values of punctuality, truthfulness, sincerity, following rules, cooperation, learning to work, getting along well with others, and inculcating a sense of responsibility are all important parts of a well-rounded education. “If you want something, you have never had, Then you have got to do something, you have never done”
